## Token Economics (Anti-Dump)

| Allocation | % | ALPHA | Vesting/Lock |
|---|---|---|---|
| Community / Mining | 50% | 500,000,000 | Earned rewards: 30-day unlock |
| Treasury / Ecosystem | 20% | 200,000,000 | Locked until grant approval |
| Team / Devs | 10% | 100,000,000 | 2-year linear vest, 6-month cliff |
| Liquidity Pool | 10% | 100,000,000 | Locked in Raydium (permanent) |
| Airdrop | 5% | 50,000,000 | 7-day lock, then linear unlock |
| Reserve | 5% | 50,000,000 | 1-year cliff, then linear |

## Sell Protection Mechanisms

1. **Reward Vesting**: Earned rewards unlock 20% immediately + 80% over 30 days
2. **Stake Requirement**: Must maintain minimum stake to earn — unstaking pauses rewards
3. **Sell Tax**: 5% tax on sells redirected to LP (if token contract supports it)
4. **Airdrop Lock**: 7-day minimum hold before airdrop tokens can be sold
5. **Team Vest**: 2-year linear vest with 6-month cliff — team can't sell early
